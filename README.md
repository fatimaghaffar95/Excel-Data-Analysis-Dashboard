# Excel-Data-Analysis-Dashboard


For a customer churn dataset, the dashboard should answer one question first:

# *Who is churning, and why?*

KPIs
1. Total Customers
2. Customers Churned	
3. Churn Rate	
4. Average Monthly Charges	
5. Average Tenure	


 📈Key findings


1. Tenure is the strongest churn predictor. Churn drops from 47.4% in the first 12 months to 9.5% at 49+ months — roughly a 5x difference.
2. Payment method matters. Customers paying by Electronic check churn at ~45%, more than double every other payment method (Bank transfer, Credit card, Mailed check all sit around 15–19%).
3. Contract type drives risk concentration. Month-to-month customers make up the large majority of churned accounts compared to one-year and two-year contracts.
4. Support friction correlates with churn. Customers with 3+ tech support tickets churn at ~74%, versus ~20% for customers with zero tickets.
5. Fiber optic internet customers churn more (~42%) than DSL (~19%) or no-internet customers (~7%)
